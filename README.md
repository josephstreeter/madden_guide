# Madden Franchise Mode Guide for PlayStation

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for mastering **Franchise Mode** in Madden on PlayStation.

**Important:** This guide is specifically designed for Franchise Mode gameplay. It does not cover Ultimate Team, online competitive modes, or other game modes.

## What Makes This Guide Different

- **PlayStation-Specific Controls:** Every technique includes exact button combinations (X, Triangle, Circle, Square, L1, L2, R1, R2, sticks)
- **Step-by-Step Execution:** Not just what to do, but HOW to execute it with detailed mechanics
- **Situational Strategy:** When and why to use each technique, not just the mechanics
- **Comprehensive Coverage:** 700+ techniques across offense, defense, special teams, and franchise management
- **Dynasty Building:** Multi-year franchise strategy for sustained success over 5-10 seasons

**Skill Levels Covered:**
- **Beginner:** New to Madden or need to learn fundamentals
- **Intermediate:** Know basics, want to improve specific skills
- **Advanced:** Competitive player seeking edge strategies (RPOs, zone blitzes, cap management)

## What You'll Learn

**On-Field Gameplay:**
- 50+ offensive plays including formations (11, 12, 13 personnel), route concepts (flood, mesh, levels), and RPOs
- 40+ defensive strategies including coverage shells (Cover 0-6), blitzing packages, user control techniques, and gap assignments
- Reading defenses pre/post-snap, identifying coverages, and exploiting weaknesses
- Hot routes, audibles, pass protection (ID the Mike, slide protection), and blocking adjustments
- Complete special teams: field goals (3-click meter), punting (coffin corner), returns, coverage, and trick plays

**Franchise Management:**
- Salary cap mastery: contract structures, restructuring, cutting players, avoiding cap hell
- Scouting and draft strategy: focus vs. spread scouting, BPA vs. need, finding gems, avoiding busts
- Player development: XP system, progression/regression curves, development traits (Star, Superstar, X-Factor)
- Trade mechanics, free agency bidding, roster construction (53-man + practice squad)
- Dynasty building: Year-by-year roadmap from rebuilding to sustained championship contention
